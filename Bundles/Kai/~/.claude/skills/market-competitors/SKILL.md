# Market Competitors Expert

Competitive design research for GRC, TPRM, and compliance market.

## Self-Improving Skill
This skill evolves as competitors are researched. Update `competitors.md` with new findings.

## Competitor Categories

| Category | Focus | Key Players |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| GRC | Governance, Risk, Compliance | ServiceNow, SAP, MetricStream |
| TPRM | Third-Party Risk Management | Prevalent, OneTrust, ProcessUnity |
| Cyber Ratings | Security Scoring | BitSight, SecurityScorecard, RiskRecon |
| Compliance | Regulatory Compliance | Vanta, Drata, Secureframe |

## Research Fields Per Competitor

Track these for each competitor:
1. Company name & website
2. Primary category
3. Products/sub-products
4. Design system (if public)
5. UI framework/tech stack
6. Primary font
7. Primary brand color
8. Navigation style (sidebar, top-nav, hybrid)
9. Mobile responsiveness
10. Dark mode support
11. Accessibility features
12. Notable UX patterns

## Differentiation Opportunities

When analyzing competitors, identify:
- **What they do well** → Consider adopting
- **What they do poorly** → Opportunity to differentiate
- **What's missing** → Innovation opportunity
- **Common patterns** → User expectation (baseline)
